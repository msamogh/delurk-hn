D Delurk HN no points · no comment counts · threads stay locked until you post

This is Hacker News with the herd muted. No points, no comment counts, no threads — so nobody's opinion reaches you before your own. Pick a story, write your take, and it's posted straight to HN as your comment. Only then does the real thread unlock, and you can see how your view stacks up.

Log in to Hacker News

Your credentials are forwarded once, directly to news.ycombinator.com over HTTPS, to obtain your HN session cookie. The server keeps nothing — the session lives only in your browser. Prefer not to trust a hosted instance? Run it locally: the whole app is one Node file.

Stuck on a captcha? Use your existing HN session instead

HN blocks password logins it thinks are automated. Skip it: log in at news.ycombinator.com in a normal tab (solving any captcha there), then hand this app the session you just created — your password never comes here.

  1. On news.ycombinator.com (logged in), open your browser's DevTools (right-click → Inspect).
  2. Application/Storage → Cookies → https://news.ycombinator.com.
  3. Copy the whole Value of the user cookie (looks like yourname&a1b2c3…).
